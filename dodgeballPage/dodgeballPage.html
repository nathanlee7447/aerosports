Experience the Thrill of Dodgeball

Get ready for heart-pounding action and non-stop fun with Dodgeball at AeroSports Oakville! Gather your friends, unleash your competitive spirit, and prepare for an adrenaline-fueled game of dodging, ducking, and diving.


How It Works

Dodgeball is a fast-paced game where two teams compete to eliminate players from the opposing team by hitting them with thrown balls while avoiding being hit themselves. At AeroSports Oakville, our state-of-the-art dodgeball courts provide the perfect arena for intense matches and thrilling showdowns. Dodge, dip, duck, dive, and dodge your way to victory!


Why You'll Love it

Dodgeball offers an exhilarating experience suitable for players of all ages and skill levels. Here's why it's a must-try:

  • High-Energy Fun: Experience the rush of adrenaline as you compete in fast-paced matches filled with strategic throws and lightning-fast dodges.

  • Team Building: Strengthen friendships and foster camaraderie as you work together with your teammates to outmaneuver and outlast the opposing team.

  • Great Workout: Dodgeball is not only incredibly fun, but it's also a fantastic way to burn calories, improve agility, and boost cardiovascular health.

  • Perfect for Parties: Whether it's a birthday celebration, corporate event, or a fun day out with friends, dodgeball is the ultimate group activity that guarantees laughter and memories.
