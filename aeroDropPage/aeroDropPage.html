Double the Thrills with Aero Drop and Rock Climbing at AeroSports Oakville!

Get ready for an adrenaline-packed experience at AeroSports Oakville! Our Aero Drop attraction not only features a thrilling free-fall with a massive 3,000-square-foot airbag but also an exciting Rock Climbing wall to test your skills before the big leap. Perfect for thrill-seekers of all ages, this area combines the best of both adventure worlds.


How Big is 1,000 Square Feet?

The airbag below the Aero Drop is a gigantic 1,000 square feet—almost the size of a small basketball court! With so much room, every jump and fall is safe, comfortable, and full of excitement. The Rock Climbing wall adds an extra layer of adventure, making it an unforgettable experience for those looking to conquer heights and land softly.


Why Kids Love the Combo of Aero Drop and Rock Climbing

For kids, the Aero Drop and Rock Climbing combo is the ultimate thrill! First, they can challenge themselves by scaling the climbing wall, feeling the excitement as they reach new heights. Then, they take the leap onto the giant airbag below, experiencing the thrill of free-fall with a soft, spacious landing. Each moment is packed with excitement, confidence-building, and memories they’ll want to relive!


Perfect for Parties and Group Events

Hosting a birthday or special event? The Aero Drop and Rock Climbing area at AeroSports Oakville is a crowd-pleaser! Guests can cheer each other on as they climb and jump, making it a fantastic choice for group fun. Whether for birthdays, team events, or family days out, this attraction adds unforgettable energy to any celebration.
