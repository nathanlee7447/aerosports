Support Sickkids: A Fun and Meaningful Experience at AeroSports Oakville

At AeroSports Oakville, we’re more than just a trampoline park—we're committed to making a positive impact in our community. Through our partnership with the SickKids Foundation, we’re helping raise funds for life-saving treatments and care for children in need. Join us for a fun, family-friendly experience that also supports a meaningful cause, and make a difference while having fun!


Why choose Aerosports Oakville?

Our indoor trampoline park is the perfect place for kids and families to get active, have fun, and give back. Whether you're from Mississauga, Oakville, Milton, Burlington, or anywhere in the Greater Toronto Area, you’ll find excitement and adventure at every jump. By visiting, you’ll also be supporting the SickKids Foundation through our ongoing events and initiatives.

  • Indoor Fun for All Ages: Our trampoline park provides a safe, clean, and exciting environment for children and families of all ages to jump, play, and stay active.
  • Support SickKids: Every visit to AeroSports Oakville helps raise money for the SickKids Foundation, ensuring that kids in need receive the best possible medical care.
  • Family-Friendly Environment: With attractions for all ages, from toddlers to teens, and activities that cater to the whole family, AeroSports Oakville is the perfect place for a fun outing with your loved ones.
  • Prime Location in the GTA: Located conveniently in Oakville, we’re easily accessible to families from Mississauga, Milton, Burlington, Etobicoke, Toronto, Brampton, Georgetown, and the rest of the GTA.

SickKids Night – A Special Event for a Special Cause

Join us every first Wednesday of the month from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM for SickKids Night at AeroSports Oakville. During this event, $10 from each jump pass sold will be donated to the SickKids Foundation to support children's health care and medical research. It’s a great way to have fun with your family while supporting a wonderful cause.


How You Can Help

By visiting AeroSports Oakville, you’re not just having fun—you’re making a difference! Your support helps fund critical research and medical treatments for children at SickKids Hospital. Whether you’re planning a fun family outing, hosting a birthday party, or just looking to stay active, every jump you take helps kids in need.

  • Jump for a Cause: Participate in SickKids Night or any of our special events, and your fun directly supports SickKids Foundation.
  • Exclusive Member Discounts: Members enjoy discounts on admission, birthday parties, merchandise, and more, while also contributing to SickKids’ mission of improving the lives of children in need.
  • Fun for the Whole Family: With a variety of attractions—such as bounce zones, dodgeball arenas, foam pits, and toddler zones—there’s something for everyone in your family to enjoy.

Visit Us Today – Make Every Jump Count!

Come experience the thrill of our indoor trampoline park and show your support for SickKids. Whether you’re local to Oakville or visiting from nearby Mississauga, Milton, Burlington, or the GTA, AeroSports Oakville is the perfect destination for family fun and charitable giving.

For more information on upcoming SickKids Nights and other events, or to learn more about how you can contribute, please visit our SickKids partnership page or contact us directly.
