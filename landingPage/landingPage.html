Birthday Parties

We feature a full assortment of birthday party packages, options and add-ons that are sure to impress jumpers of all ages. Aerosports Oakville - Mississauga hosts 3 party rooms as well as 2 large corporate rooms that fits up to 150 guests.

Groups & Events

AeroSports Trampoline Park Oakville is the perfect venue for hosting unforgettable group events! Whether you're planning a school field trip, a corporate party, or a holiday celebration, our facility offers a unique and exciting environment that caters to all ages.
